Surveys are recommended based on:
  • Theoretical basis and validation. 'Yes' and 'indirect' values in lower rows.
  • Permission to resuse with modification and no cost. Green cells in Reusage row.
  • Availabiity of relevant benchmarks. For example, the AHRQ provides benchmarks, but the NIOSH does not. Green cells in Benchmarks row.
  • Brevity. For example, when measuring engagement, the UWES-3 is shorter that the UWES-9 and with adequate ( r > 0.7 per Nunnally & Bernstein, 1994) correlation to the UWES-9 (Schaufeli, 2017).

  • Integrative model of human growth at work. Source: Spreitzer GM, Porath CM. Self-Determination as a Nutriment for Thriving: Building an Integrative Model of Human Growth at Work Page. In: Gagne M, editor. The Oxford Handbook of Work Engagement, Motivation, and Self-Determination Theory. 1 edition.   • Complexity leadership theory. Source: Uhl-Bien M, Arena M. Leadership for organizational adaptability: A theoretical synthesis and integrative framework. The Leadership Quarterly. 2018;29(1):89-104. Available from: http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S104898431730111X

  • EHR burden (details at HealthIT.gov and WikiDoc).

    • EHR exposure be the proximal antecedent and lack of autonomy or voice by the front line in designing EHR workflow.

  • Peer/reciprocal learning (Leykum. PMIDs22310483,21345225).
    • Associated with: engagement and thriving
    • The distal antecedent may be creation of membership across a workforce

    • Scales include:
      1. Engagement to work on the 2018 survey is questions 2a, 2b, 2c (Based on the three validated dimensions of the UWES-9 although question 2a did not make the final model (DOI 10.1177/0013164405282471)
      2. Engagement to organzation (advocacy) on the 2018 survey is questions 21c, 21d (Based on Institute for Employment Studies (IES) (10.1177/0013164405282471)
      3. Engagement to organzation (involvement) on the 2018 survey is questions 4a, 4b, 4c, 4d (Based on 'empowerment' in the NHS Constitution)
      4. Openness on the 2018 survey is questions 17b, 17d (17d is a paraphrase), 18a, 18b (PMID 31059370). Selection and derivation of of questions not clear.
      5. Freedom to Speak Up Index on the 2018 survey is questions 17a, 17b, 18a, 18b (PMID 31660523). Selection and derivation of of questions not clear.
    • Usage
      • Engagement is affected by differences in job types (managers have more engagement) and Trust types (Ambulance Trusts have less engagement) (West and Dawson, 2012)

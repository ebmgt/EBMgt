  • Mortality at Trusts
    • ummary Hospital-level Mortality Indicator (SHMI) "reports on mortality at trust level...The SHMI is the ratio between the actual number of patients who die following hospitalisation at the trust and the number that would be expected to die on the basis of average England figures, given the characteristics of the patients treated"
    • SHMI accounting for deprivation/disparity
  • Adult Inpatient Surveys from Staff Surveys administered by the Picker Institute
    • Content
      • Scales include:
        1. Engagement to work (Measuring employee engagement and interpreting survey results) on the 2018 survey is questions 2a, 2b, 2c (Based on the three validated dimensions of the UWES-9 although question 2a did not make the final model (DOI 10.1177/0013164405282471)
        2. Engagement to organization (advocacy) (Measuring employee engagement and interpreting survey results) on the 2018 survey is questions 21c, 21d (Based on Institute for Employment Studies (IES)
        3. Engagement to organization (involvement) (Measuring employee engagement and interpreting survey results) on the 2018 survey is questions 4a, 4b, 4c, 4d (Based on 'empowerment' in the NHS Constitution)
        4. Openness on the 2018 survey is questions 17b, 17d (17d is a paraphrase), 18a, 18b (PMID 31059370). Selection and derivation of of questions not clear.
